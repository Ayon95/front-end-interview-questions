Static vs Relative vs Absolute vs Fixed positioning

I have static positioning which is the default or normal positioning. I am positioned according to the normal flow.

I am relatively positioned, i.e. positioned relative to my normal position. I am offset from my normal position.

I am absolutely positioned. I am the child of a relatively-positioned element. I caused the height of my parent to collapse.

I have fixed positioning. I am positioned relative to the initial containing block (the viewport).
